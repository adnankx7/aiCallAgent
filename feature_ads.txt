---------------------------------
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
---------------------------------

Q: Can I upgrade my plan later?
A: Yes, you can upgrade from Free to Featured or Featured PRO at any time during your listing period.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payment methods like JazzCash and Easypaisa.

Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Refunds are available within 24 hours of purchase if your ad hasn't gone live yet.

Q: How quickly will my ad go live?
A: Free ads go live within 24 hours, while Featured and Featured PRO ads are activated immediately after payment.
